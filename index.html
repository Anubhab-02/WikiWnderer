Anubhab Kalita

Anubhab is a student pursuing his Bachelor's degree in Mathematics who has invented a new sport called “Little ball tennis”. This sport combines various aspects of badminton, table tennis, tennis, pickleball and paddle ball. It has spin, pace, skill and ease to it. Little ball tennis is a fun and exciting sport that can be enjoyed by people of all ages and skill levels.
